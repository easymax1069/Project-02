Reputation Ranking

The Reputation Ranking is an annually-published list showcasing the cities which have the best hotel reputations - either worldwide or in specific countries. Reputation Ranking release can also be sent during the summer and winter months with a sole focus on holiday destinations.

HPI (Hotel Price Index)

The Hotel Price Index is released monthly and forecasts the average hotel prices of major European, North American, South American and Asian ciites. It shows the overnight accomodation prices based on a standard double room.
